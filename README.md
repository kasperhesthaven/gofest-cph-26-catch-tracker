# Copenhagen Pokemon GO Fest Catch Tracker '26

A simple offline & mobile optimized PWA for tracking catches and shinies during Pokémon GO Fest 2026 in Copenhagen.

### How to use it:
1. Open this link: [https://kasperhesthaven.github.io/gofest-cph-26-catch-tracker/](https://kasperhesthaven.github.io/gofest-cph-26-catch-tracker/)
2. **On iPhone:** Tap the "Share" button at the bottom and hit "Add to Home Screen".
3. **On Android:** Tap the three dots in the top right corner and hit "Add to Home screen".
4. Just open it from your home screen like a normal app.
